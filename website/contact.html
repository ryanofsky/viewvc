Contacting Us

Please send any comments, questions, or suggestions to the ViewCVS mailing list. There is also a mailing list specifically for ViewCVS developers.

Archives of these lists are available at the following locations:

  • ViewCVS (lyra.org)
  • ViewCVS developers (lyra.org)
  • combined ViewCVS lists (archive.netbsd.se)

ViewCVS is an Open Source project, and all contributions are welcome.
